<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to the Perfect Counter">

Executing a successful counter-attack is the hallmark of a skilled player. This **knife duels guide** recommends practicing the "Flash-Step Counter" to climb the ranks quickly. This technique relies on the server's hit-registration logic and requires precise execution of movement and camera control.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Bait">
    Approach the opponent's "danger zone" (just outside their reach) and tap your forward key. This usually triggers their instinct to swing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Retreat">
    The moment you see their arm move, tap your backward key or dash button. You want their knife to miss by the smallest possible margin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Pivot">
    As their swing animation enters the "recovery" phase, immediately lunge forward. Aim for the torso or head to maximize hit probability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Reset">
    After landing the hit, do not stay in range. Immediately strafe to the side to avoid a "revenge swing" and reset your stamina.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Mechanic">
Mastering Step 2 is the hardest part. If you retreat too early, the opponent won't swing. If you retreat too late, you take damage. Practice in the training lobby to find the "sweet spot."
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Maneuvers and Survival">

Offense wins rounds, but defense wins tournaments. In Knife Duels, survival is often about "active defense"—using your movement to make yourself an impossible target. Static blocking is rarely effective against high-tier players who know how to bypass guards. Instead, focus on these three defensive archetypes.

| Defense Type | Technique | Best Against | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Ghost** | Constant erratic jumping/crouching | Heavy weapons | High |
| **The Wall** | Perfect parry timing | Light spammers | Medium |
| **The Dancer** | Large circular strafing | Standard daggers | Low |

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
Server latency (Ping) heavily affects parry timing. If your ping is over 80ms, rely more on "The Dancer" movement style rather than trying to time perfect parries.
</Callout>

### Advanced Positioning Tactics
1. **Corner Trapping**: Force your opponent toward the edge of the arena to limit their strafing options.
2. **High Ground Advantage**: If the arena has elevation, attacking from above increases your reach slightly due to the game's hitbox calculation.
3. **Stamina Management**: Never let your stamina bar drop below 20%. A player with no stamina is a player who cannot dash out of danger.
