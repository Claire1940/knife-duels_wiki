<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step: Setting Up Your Controller">

If you are new to using a gamepad for Roblox PvP, following a structured setup process is vital. This ensures your sensitivity and button layouts are calibrated for the fast-paced nature of Knife Duels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Roblox Controller Support">
    Connect your controller via USB or Bluetooth. Open the Roblox settings menu in-game and ensure the 'Control Mode' is set to 'Controller' or 'Automatic'.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adjust Camera Sensitivity">
    Navigate to the settings and set your camera sensitivity. For controller players, a higher sensitivity is recommended (around 6-8) to allow for quick 180-degree turns during backstab attempts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Calibrate Deadzones">
    If your controller has stick drift, adjust the deadzone settings in your console or PC software. You want the smallest deadzone possible to ensure immediate movement response.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Practice in Training Mode">
    Before entering 1v1 matches, spend 10 minutes in the Training or Free-For-All mode. Practice hitting the 'Attack' button while strafing in circles to get used to the analog acceleration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
