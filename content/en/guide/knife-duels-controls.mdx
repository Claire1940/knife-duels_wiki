<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Duel Progression">

Once you have memorized the **knife duels controls**, you must learn how to apply them in a live match. Combat isn't about button mashing; it is a game of baiting and punishing. Every swing has a "wind-up" phase and a "recovery" phase. If you swing and miss, you are stuck in recovery, making you a sitting duck for an experienced opponent.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Approach your opponent using the **Shift** key to maintain speed. Do not attack immediately. Instead, enter their threat range and immediately back out to see how they react.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Baiting the Swing">
    Most beginner players will panic-click **LMB** as soon as you get close. By using your movement controls to stay just outside their reach, you can force them to miss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Executing the Punish">
    As soon as the opponent's knife animation begins its recovery, move in and tap **LMB**. This is the "punish window" where they cannot block or attack back.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset and Defensive Stance">
    After landing a hit, immediately hold **Ctrl** (or tap Block on mobile) and retreat. This prevents them from landing a "trade" hit, ensuring you keep your health advantage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Winning Strategy">
