To truly excel in Knife Duels, you must move beyond basic throwing. Map awareness is the difference between a 1.0 K/D and a 5.0 K/D. Most maps feature "power positions"—areas with high visibility but plenty of cover.

**Key Tactical Considerations:**
- **Flanking**: Most players focus on the center lane. Using side routes allows you to get within stabbing range without being spotted.
- **The "Jump Throw"**: Jumping while throwing can give your knife a slightly different trajectory, often bypassing low cover that opponents think is safe.
- **Handling Hackers**: As a new game, Knife Duels currently faces challenges with exploiters. If you encounter a player with "infinite reach" or "teleport stabs," it is best to leave the lobby and find a fresh match.
