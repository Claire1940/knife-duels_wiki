To truly excel in the **knife duels trading community**, you need to understand "flipping." Flipping involves buying an item at a low price from someone who needs coins quickly and selling it at a higher price to a collector.

### The "Add" System
In high-tier trading, items rarely have equal value. Players use "Adds" (smaller items) to balance a trade.
- **Overpaying**: Giving more value than you receive to get a highly desirable item.
- **Underpaying**: Receiving more value than you give, usually by trading multiple low-tier items for one high-tier item.
