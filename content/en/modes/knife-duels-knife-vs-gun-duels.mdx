<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Movement Guide to Counter Ranged Threats">

Closing the gap against a ranged player requires a systematic, disciplined approach. You cannot simply hold down the forward key and hope for the best. A skilled opponent will easily predict your trajectory and land their shots or throws. Instead, you must implement a structured tactical approach that combines baiting, erratic movement, and precise execution.

By utilizing the proper keybinds and inputs, PC and Mobile players can maximize their movement efficiency. The goal is to make your character's hitbox as unpredictable as possible, forcing the opponent to miss their initial attack and creating a window of vulnerability that you can exploit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Bait and Assess">
    Identify the opponent's weapon type and observe their throwing or shooting rhythm. Stay at the edge of their effective range to bait out their primary attack without putting yourself in immediate danger.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Erratic Strafe Patterns">
    Combine rapid left and right strafes with jumps to break the opponent's lock-on. Avoid predictable jumping patterns, as experienced players can easily track and punish players who jump too frequently in a straight line.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Sprint Close">
    Activate your sprint mechanic (Shift on PC) to rapidly decrease the distance once the opponent misses their attack or enters a recovery animation. Maintain a diagonal path rather than running straight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Defend">
    Deliver a swift primary strike (LMB on PC) once you are within weapon range. Immediately follow up by holding your defensive block (Ctrl on PC) to mitigate any immediate counter-strikes or panic attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Platform | Keybind / Input | Combat Action | Tactical Application |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| PC | Shift | Sprint | Close distance rapidly and chase fleeing targets |
| PC | Space | Jump | Evade low attacks and disrupt enemy aiming lines |
| PC | Ctrl | Block | Mitigate incoming damage and prepare for counters |
| PC | Left Mouse Button (LMB) | Primary Attack | Strike with precision once within range |
| Mobile | Virtual Joystick | Character Movement | Maintain continuous erratic strafing |
| Mobile | Attack Button | Swing Knife | Tap rapidly during close-quarters engagements |

<Callout type="tip" title="Vertical Spacing">
While sprinting is your main tool for closing distance, do not neglect jumping. Jumping mid-sprint changes your vertical hitbox, making it incredibly difficult for ranged opponents to score precise headshots or direct projectile hits.
</Callout>

Mastering this sequence ensures that you dictate the pace of the engagement. By forcing the ranged player to react to your movement rather than letting them control the distance, you place them at a severe psychological and tactical disadvantage.
