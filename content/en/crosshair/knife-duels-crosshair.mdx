To get the most out of your **knife duels crosshair**, you must master the fundamental controls. The way you move directly impacts where your crosshair is pointing. In **Knife Duels**, movement and aiming are two sides of the same coin.

**Key Combat Controls for 2026:**
- **LMB (Left Mouse Button)**: Primary attack. Timing this with your crosshair placement is vital.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Combat Strategies & Spacing">

Winning a duel isn't just about clicking; it's about where you are standing when you click. The **knife duels crosshair** is most effective when you understand "spacing"—the distance between you and the enemy's blade.

**Combat Strategy Comparison:**

| Strategy | Focus | Best Against | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Baiting** | Forcing an enemy to swing and miss | Aggressive players | **Low** |
| **Aggressive Rush** | Overwhelming with speed | Slower weapons | Medium |
| **Counter-Striking** | Blocking then immediately attacking | Predictable players | Medium |
| **Jungle Strafe** | Using jumps and erratic movement | Precise aimers | **High** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Button Mashing">
Mashing the attack button creates a predictable rhythm. Experienced players will time their blocks to your clicks. Instead, wait for a clear opening where their crosshair is turned away from you.
</Callout>

**Advanced Tips for 2026 Gameplay:**
- **Watch the Shoulders**: An opponent's character model usually leans or shifts slightly before a swing. Use this as a visual cue to reposition your crosshair.
- **Corner Trapping**: Use the arena boundaries to limit where an opponent can dodge, making your aiming job much easier.
- **Verticality**: In maps with stairs or platforms, attacking from above often bypasses an enemy's block because their crosshair is leveled horizontally.

<Callout type="info" title="Map Awareness">
Always be aware of the arena layout. Getting backed into a corner makes it impossible to strafe, effectively rendering your movement-based aiming useless.
</Callout>
