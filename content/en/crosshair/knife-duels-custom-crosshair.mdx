<Section icon={Map} title="How to Set Up Your Custom Crosshair">

Setting up a **knife duels custom crosshair** is a straightforward process, but it requires you to have the correct Asset IDs from the Roblox marketplace. Since Knife Duels is built on the Roblox engine, it utilizes the standard image ID system for UI customization.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find a Crosshair ID">
    Browse the Roblox Create/Marketplace section for "Crosshair" or "Reticle" decals. Copy the numerical ID found in the URL of the asset (e.g., `rbxassetid://123456789`).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access In-Game Settings">
    Launch Knife Duels and look for the **Settings** gear icon, usually located on the left or bottom of the main menu screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Input the ID">
    Locate the field labeled "Custom Crosshair ID" or "Reticle ID." Paste your copied numerical string into the box and press Enter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Adjust Visuals">
    Fine-tune the size and transparency. A size between 10-20 pixels is recommended for most 1080p monitors to maintain a balance between visibility and precision.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Asset Loading">
If your crosshair appears as a gray box or doesn't load, ensure the ID is set to "Public" on the Roblox site. Some IDs may be deleted or moderated over time.
</Callout>
