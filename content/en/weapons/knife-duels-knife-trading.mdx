<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Trade Safely and Avoid Scams">

As the trading scene grows, so does the risk of encountering bad actors. Safe trading requires vigilance, patience, and a strict adherence to security protocols. Many scammers rely on psychological pressure, rushing their victims to accept trades before they have time to thoroughly inspect the items in the trading window.

The most common exploit in weapon trading is the "quick-switch" scam, where a player places a high-value legendary skin in the trade window, waits for you to offer your items, and then quickly swaps their item for a common skin right before clicking accept. To prevent this, always take a breath and double-check every single slot before finalizing any transaction.

| Scam Type | Method of Attack | Risk Level | Countermeasure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quick-Switch** | Swapping a legendary skin for a common lookalike | High | Wait 5 seconds after any change before clicking accept |
| **Cross-Trading** | Offering Robux or external items outside Roblox | Extreme | Only trade items within the official in-game window |
| **Value Spoofing** | Lying about a skin's actual tier or demand level | Medium | Consult our updated 2026 value tier list before trading |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Trade Request">
    Send a trade request directly through the in-game player list inside a secure public lobby. Avoid trading in private servers with players you do not trust.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verify Item Rarity and Names">
